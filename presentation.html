Problem Context

Why This Problem Matters

New investors are increasingly vulnerable to fraud and data misuse in the micro-investment space

🎭 Investment Fraud Epidemic

First-time investors are primary targets of sophisticated fraud schemes. They lack experience to identify red flags and often trust platforms blindly.

  • Fake investment apps promising unrealistic returns
  • Ponzi schemes disguised as legitimate platforms
  • Social engineering through referral programs

🔓 Data Security Concerns

Many micro-investment apps collect sensitive KYC data but fail to implement proper security measures.

  • Weak encryption or plain-text storage
  • No clear consent mechanisms
  • Third-party data sharing without disclosure

Security Implementation

How We Protect Investors

Security is not an afterthought — it's built into every layer of the ZAP platform. From encrypted storage to blockchain verification, we ensure complete protection of user data and assets.

🔐

Data Protection

  • Encrypted storage: All KYC data encrypted using AES-256 before storage
  • Explicit consent: Checkbox confirmations for each data usage type
  • No sharing: Zero third-party data sharing policy
  • Session security: JWT tokens with short expiry and refresh mechanism
🤖

AI Fraud Detection

  • Device fingerprint: Track device patterns to detect account takeover
  • Cash-out alerts: Flag unusual withdrawal patterns
  • Referral analysis: Detect pyramid scheme indicators
  • Risk scoring: Real-time AI assessment of transaction risk
⛓️

Blockchain Audit

  • Immutable logs: Every transaction recorded on-chain
  • Smart guards: Rate limiting and validation in contracts
  • Verification: Users can verify transactions on Etherscan
  • Transparency: Open audit trail for regulatory compliance
Fraud-Signal Layer

Z-Guard AI Engine

Addressing Deliverable 3: Device checks, unusual cash-out patterns, suspicious referrals

🤖

Z-Guard

Our proprietary AI fraud detection engine monitors user behavior in real-time, identifying patterns that indicate fraudulent activity before damage occurs.

📱 Device Fingerprinting

Tracks device characteristics, IP patterns, and login locations. Alerts when account is accessed from new/unusual devices to prevent account takeover attacks.

💸 Unusual Cash-Out Detection

AI monitors withdrawal patterns. Flags sudden large withdrawals, multiple small withdrawals (structuring), or withdrawals inconsistent with investment history.

🔗 Suspicious Referral Analysis

Detects pyramid/ponzi scheme patterns in referral networks. Flags accounts with abnormal referral trees or incentive-driven signup patterns.

📊 Real-Time Risk Scoring

Every transaction receives a risk score (0-100). High-risk transactions require additional verification or are blocked automatically.
